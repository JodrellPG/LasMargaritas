¡Welcome to the PUEDO Program!


Programa Unificado de Educación y Desarrollo para Oportunidades. Join us in transforming lives and opening doors for tomorrow through education and development.

Explore Your Potential

We believe that every student deserves the opportunity to discover and develop their potential. At Las Margaritas Foundation, we are dedicated to providing underserved students with a comprehensive education, including reading, reading comprehension, and extracurricular activities such as STEAM, chess, and much more.


Based on our commitment to nurturing the academic growth of underserved students, we have launched the PUEDO program. This program offers tutoring and academic support to participants from underserved communities, guiding them toward admission to prestigious institutions like Instituto Politécnico Loyola (IPL). Discover your potential and pursue your dreams with PUEDO.

Changing Lives and Opening Doors for Tomorrow

We believe in breaking barriers and changing lives. Through collaboration with Nathalie Brito, we're demystifying perceptions about IPL and empowering students from our centers to access high-quality education, enabling them to explore their potential and secure a brighter future for themselves and their families, breaking the cycle of poverty. Together, we're revolutionizing education and creating opportunities for underserved communities. Join us in shaping a brighter future for all.


Through a tailored approach to early childhood development, our program focuses on fostering a strong foundation in key areas such as cognitive skills, social-emotional growth, and academic readiness. By engaging children in stimulating activities and interactive learning experiences, we aim to cultivate their curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking abilities from an early age.

Academic Support and Scholarships

We believe that every student deserves the opportunity to discover and develop their potential. At Las Margaritas Foundation, we are dedicated to providing underserved students with a comprehensive education, including reading, reading comprehension, and extracurricular activities such as STEAM, ESL (English as a Second Language), chess, and much more.


Las Margaritas Foundation provides comprehensive support to students through tutoring to improve their chances of passing admission tests. Additionally, scholarships are available to assist with educational expenses. Even after admission, we continue to support students academically at the center, leveraging the resources of the foundation to ensure their success.
