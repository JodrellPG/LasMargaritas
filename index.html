We are on a mission to elevate our community

Mission

Our mission is to serve the needs of the community of San Cristobal, in the Dominican Republic. We are dedicated to helping underserved children have the opportunity to become leaders who bring positive change to their community.

Vision

We are guided by our dedication to helping children and youth from marginalized groups to have the opportunity to become leaders who bring about positive change in their community

Donations

Your kindness enables us to pursue our mission of providing opportunities to marginalized groups of children, families, and communities we serve. Your donations are instrumental in making a real difference in their lives.

Our Programs

University scholarships

Thanks to your support, our scholarship recipients have obtained their bachelor's degrees from various universities including UTESA, O&M, Eugenio Maria de Hostos, UAPA, and UASD, with technical courses also offered, supported by donations.

STEM

Centers I and II are tackling academic challenges with a STEM program and collaborating with Nathalie Brito to provide technology training (programming languages, robotics, etc.) by engaging students from IPL and universities.

Literacy

Building a love of reading is at the core of our work. We have designed a rigorous reading curriculum based on the science of reading that enables us to teach not only our young students but also adults within the community who are illiterate.

Summer Camp

Las Margaritas Foundation, established in 2010 as a summer camp, now offers diverse activities and educational outreach in Madre Vieja Sur and Las Galeras, positively impacting children and youth under the leadership of educators.
