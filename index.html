What we are doing?

We are in a mission to help the helpless

Mission

Our mission is to serve the needs of the community of San Cristobal, in the Dominican Republic. We are dedicated to helping deprived children have the opportunity to become leaders who bring positive change to their community.

Donations

Your kindness enables us to pursue our mission of providing opportunities to underprivileged children, families, and communities we serve. Your donations are instrumental in making a real difference in their lives.

Vision

We are guided by our dedication to helping children and youth from underprivileged backgrounds to have the opportunity to become leaders who bring about positive change in their community

Our Programs

University scholarships

The program, initially for foundation participants, has helped six beneficiaries graduate from UTESA, O&M, Eugenio Maria de Hostos, University of the Third Age, and UASD, with technical courses also offered, supported by donations.

STEM

Centers I and II are tackling academic challenges with a STEAM program and collaborating with Nathalie Brito to provide technology training (programming languages, robotics, etc.) by engaging students from IPL and universities.

Literacy

Available to the entire population, both young and adults. These classes are free and taught by our collaborators. We have been able to provide literacy education to elderly individuals as well as children just starting out.

Summer Camp

Las Margaritas Foundation, established in 2010 as a summer camp, now offers diverse activities and educational outreach in Madre Vieja Sur and Las Galeras, positively impacting children and youth under the leadership of educators Tania and Josh.
